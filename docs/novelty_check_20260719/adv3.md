### Verdict

**CLAIM SURVIVES.** The strongest omissions demonstrate wearable-gaze retrieval, mobile gaze-guided search, or gaze grounding in a shared world frame—but none demonstrates all three claimed pillars together. For publication, clarify that “outside the robot’s view” means **at command/reference-resolution time**, and qualify the priority statement with “to our knowledge.”
